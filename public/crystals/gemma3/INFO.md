# THE TRIAD

**Architecture:** Gemma 3 (Multimodal)
**Shape:** The Triple Helix
**Concept:** Native Multimodality

A revolutionary architecture that interleaves Text, Audio, and Video processing layers natively.
Released by Google in **2025**, Gemma 3 abandoned the "connector" approach for a truly unified neural structure.

**Use Cases:**
*   **Omni-Interaction:** Seeing, hearing, and speaking simultaneously.
*   **Video Understanding:** Analyzing complex visual temporal dynamics.
*   **Real-time Voice:** Latency-free conversational AI.
