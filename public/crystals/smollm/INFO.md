# THE POCKET BRAIN

**Architecture:** SmolLM2 (360M)
**Shape:** The Pocket Brain
**Concept:** Edge Intelligence

While giants dominate the cloud, SmolLM2 brings intelligence to the edge.
Optimized for mobile devices, it represents the 2025 trend: AI Everywhere.

**Use Cases:**
*   **Mobile Prediction:** Smart keyboard autocorrect and next-sentence prediction.
*   **Privacy-First:** Summarizing notifications locally without cloud data transfer.
*   **IoT Control:** Understanding natural language commands on smart speakers.
