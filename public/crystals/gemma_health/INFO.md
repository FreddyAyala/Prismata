# THE CADUCEUS

**Architecture:** BioGPT / Gemma Health
**Shape:** The Medical Helix
**Concept:** Specialized Knowledge

A model whose entire existence is dedicated to Biology and Medicine.
Unlike generalist models, every neuron here has "read" PubMed and medical journals.

**Use Cases:**
*   **Diagnosis Support:** Analyzing patient notes and suggesting differentials.
*   **Drug Discovery:** Predicting protein folding and drug interactions.
*   **Literature Mining:** Extracting insights from millions of papers.
