# THE ADVERSARIES

**Architecture:** DCGAN (Deep Convolutional GAN)
**Shape:** The Dual Core
**Concept:** Adversarial Equilibrium

One crystal representing two minds at war.
The **Generator** (Chaos) tries to fool the **Discriminator** (Order).
Released in **2015**, DCGAN was the first stable architecture for generating images.

**Use Cases:**
*   **Image Generation:** The grandfather of Midjourney and DALL-E.
*   **Super Resolution:** Enhancing blurry images.
