# THE OPEN STANDARD

**Architecture:** Gemma 2 (Dense Decoder)
**Shape:** The Obsidian Monolith
**Concept:** Knowledge Compression

A highly optimized 2B parameter model. Dense, efficient, and surprisingly capable for its size.
Released by Google DeepMind in **2024**, it represents a shift towards high-performance open weights.

**Use Cases:**
*   **RAG Applications:** Efficiently retrieving answers from local documents.
*   **Analysis:** Summarizing long reports and academic papers.
*   **Safe Deployment:** Built with rigorous safety standards for commercial use.
